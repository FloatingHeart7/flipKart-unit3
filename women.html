Tees are the popular name for T-shirts which get their name based on the T-shape of the body and sleeves. These are worn across age groups because of the comfort they offer. Most men indulge in this clothing because of the wide assortment of designs and ease of wearing. Various brands fashion these in a range of comfortable fabrics like cotton, hosiery, polyester, nylon, and even viscose. You can also find an exclusive collection made of khadi silk. These are more suited to formal events or festive occasions. Adidas, Flying Machine, HRX by Hrithik Roshan, Levi’s, and Jack & Jones are some of the favourite brands among several individuals as they provide an extensive selection.
